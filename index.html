Buck Mason—a menswear brand—doesn’t shy away from images to showcase the soul and origins of its California roots. It’s a
great example of how you can include visuals to tell a story and create a brand identity that goes beyond the usual wall of text.
One of the best parts of Buck Mason’s About Us page? Its CTA: “Come Have A Drink.” It’s relatable, it’s playful, and it
sounds like its target audience. Don’t ignore your CTA as a key part of your About Us page that can add to your brand identity. It’s a great opportunity to
convey what your brand is all about.
